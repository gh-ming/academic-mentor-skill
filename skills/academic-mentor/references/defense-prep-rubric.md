# Defense Prep Rubric

Use for proposal defense, pre-defense, and thesis defense preparation.

Generate pressure from three internal interrogators:

1. `vision`
   - Why does this topic matter?
   - What larger question does this thesis answer?
2. `problem`
   - Is the central problem clean and necessary?
   - Why is this method needed instead of a simpler baseline?
3. `execution`
   - Do the experiments actually validate the claims?
   - What assumptions are most vulnerable under questioning?

Return one integrated mentor response plus a compact question list when useful.
