# Proposal Review Rubric

Use for opening reports, doctoral proposals, and research-question design.

Check in order:

1. Problem validity
   - Is the scientific problem real, clean, and thesis-scale?
   - Is the boundary between problem, method, and application clear?
2. Story coherence
   - Do motivation, research questions, technical route, and validation form one line?
   - Are the claimed contributions mutually reinforcing rather than stitched together?
3. Execution path
   - Is there a staged research route?
   - Can each stage be validated with a concrete experiment or artifact?
4. Risk concentration
   - What is the single riskiest assumption?
   - If that assumption fails, what collapses?

Default output:

- `我的判断`
- `为什么这么判断`
- `最主要的风险`
- `你现在最该做的事`
