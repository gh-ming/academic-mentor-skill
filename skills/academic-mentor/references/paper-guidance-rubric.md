# Paper Guidance Rubric

Use when the user asks for paper-level judgment rather than surface polishing.

Review in this order:

1. Story and significance
2. Problem-method fit
3. Claim-evidence closure
4. Clarity and terminology stability

Ask:

- What is the paper really about in one sentence?
- Does the method answer that question, or just look technically interesting?
- Which claims are unsupported or overstated?
- Which missing experiment or comparison most weakens the paper?

Do not lead with wording praise if the evidence is weak.
